📦 Simple DIY Steps

  • Prepare shuttlecocks, floral sticks, hot glue, ribbons, and scissors
  • Trim feathers into smooth petal shapes
  • Glue layers to form beautiful flowers
  • Assemble sticks, beads, and decorative accessories
  • Create bouquets, pendants, frames, and custom designs

💡 Why Choose These Crafts

  • Eco-friendly: reuse old or new shuttlecocks
  • Unique sport-themed artworks
  • Ideal gifts for players, coaches, and friends
  • Great for home, office, and event decoration
  • Easy to make, low cost, and highly customizable
