📌 About the Crafts

Badminton crafts are handmade artworks created using shuttlecocks, either new or used. They are delicate, meaningful, and full of sporty style. These crafts can be used as decorations, pendants, bouquets, souvenirs, and personalized gifts.

📦 Simple DIY Tutorial

  • Prepare materials: shuttlecocks, floral sticks, hot glue, ribbons, beads, scissors
  • Trim shuttlecock feathers into petal shapes
  • Glue pieces together to form flower shapes
  • Attach sticks and decorate with ribbons and beads
